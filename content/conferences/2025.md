# Playing Futures 2025

## Conference Overview

The inaugural Playing Futures conference will establish our community of researchers, practitioners, and thinkers exploring the intersection of play and emerging technologies.

## Theme: "Foundations of Playful Futures"

This first conference focuses on establishing foundational understanding and building our research community around play-based approaches to technology and future-making.
