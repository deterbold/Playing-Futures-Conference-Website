## Travel information

Copenhagen is easily accessible by [plane](https://www.cph.dk), [train](https://www.dsb.dk), or bus.

## Transport

Copenhagen is easy to navigate by public transportation. The [conference venue](https://www.literaturhaus.dk) is on the [5C](https://dinoffentligetransport.dk/planlaeg-din-rejse/koereplaner-for-bus-og-havnebus/koereplan?line=5C) bus route, one of the most popular in Copenhagen. Another alternative to get to the venue is to take the [Metro](https://m.dk/da/) to Nørrebros Runddel. Information about public transportation fares can be found in [Rejseplanen](https://www.rejseplanen.dk/webapp-nextgen/explore?zoom=15&lng=12.58859805799625&lat=55.65860891452101).

Copenhagen is best experienced by bike, and [Donkey Republic](https://www.donkey.bike/da/cities/copenhagen) is one of the options for temporary bike rentals.
