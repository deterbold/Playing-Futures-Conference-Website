# About Playing Futures

Playing Futures is an interdisciplinary conference that brings together researchers, practitioners, designers, and thinkers to explore the role of play in shaping our technological future.

## Mission

Our mission is to foster dialogue and collaboration around how play-based approaches can inform the development of emerging technologies and help us envision more engaging, inclusive, and human-centered futures.

## Conference Focus Areas

- **Play and Technology**: Examining how playful interactions shape technology design and adoption
- **Future Scenarios**: Using play as a method for exploring and prototyping future possibilities  
- **Digital Play**: Understanding play in digital environments and virtual spaces
- **Social Innovation**: How play can drive collaborative problem-solving and social change
- **Ethics and Play**: Considering the ethical implications of play in technology development

## Who Should Attend

- Researchers in game studies, human-computer interaction, and future studies
- Technology designers and developers
- Educators and learning experience designers
- Policy makers and social innovators
- Anyone interested in the intersection of play and future technology
