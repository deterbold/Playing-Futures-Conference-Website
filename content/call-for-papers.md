# Call for Papers - Playing Futures Conference

We invite submissions for papers, workshops, panels, and interactive demonstrations that explore the intersection of play, technology, and future possibilities.

## Submission Topics

We welcome submissions on (but not limited to) the following topics:

### Research Papers
- Theoretical frameworks for understanding play in technological contexts
- Empirical studies on play and technology adoption
- Historical perspectives on play and technological development
- Cross-cultural studies of play and technology

### Practice Papers
- Case studies of play-based technology design
- Methodologies for incorporating play into design processes
- Tools and techniques for playful interaction design
- Evaluation methods for playful technologies

### Position Papers
- Critical perspectives on play and technology
- Ethical considerations in playful technology design
- Future visions for play-based technologies
- Policy implications of play in digital spaces

## Submission Guidelines

### Paper Types
- **Full Papers**: 6-8 pages (including references)
- **Short Papers**: 3-4 pages (including references)
- **Workshop Proposals**: 2-3 pages
- **Panel Proposals**: 2-3 pages
- **Interactive Demonstrations**: 2-3 pages

### Format
All submissions should follow the ACM format and be submitted as PDF files.

### Review Process
All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review process.
